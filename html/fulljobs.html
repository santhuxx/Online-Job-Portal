Full-time Jobs



Restaurant Manager

A full-time restaurant manager is responsible for overseeing the daily operations of a restaurant. They manage staff, handle customer service, and ensure compliance with health and safety regulations.

Average salary: $3,500-$7,000/month

Sous Chef

A full-time sous chef works closely with the head chef, managing the kitchen operations, and overseeing food preparation. They ensure that dishes are executed to high standards, assist in menu planning, and supervise the kitchen staff.

Average salary: $3,000-$5,500/month

Financial Analyst

A full-time financial analyst evaluates financial data and provides insights to assist in decision-making. They analyze market trends, prepare financial reports, and make recommendations for investment strategies or cost-cutting measures.

Average salary: $4,000-$7,500/month

Project Manager


A full-time project manager leads and coordinates projects from initiation to completion. They develop project plans, allocate resources, track progress, and ensure timely delivery within budgetary constraints.

Average salary: $4,500-$8,000/month

Operations Manager

A full-time operations manager oversees the day-to-day operations of a company or department. They manage resources, optimize processes, ensure quality control, and strive for operational efficiency and productivity.

Average salary: $4,500-$8,500/month

Nurse Practitioner

A full-time nurse practitioner provides advanced nursing care, diagnoses illnesses, and prescribes medications. They collaborate with physicians, educate patients on healthcare management, and promote overall well-being.

Average salary: $5,000-$9,000/month
