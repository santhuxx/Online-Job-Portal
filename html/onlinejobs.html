Online Jobs




Virtual Assistant


A virtual assistant provides administrative support remotely to businesses or individuals. They handle tasks such as email management, scheduling appointments, data entry, and social media management.

Average salary: $12-$25/hr

Content Writer


A content writer creates engaging and informative written content for websites, blogs, and social media platforms. They research topics, write articles, and optimize content for search engines.

Average salary: $15-$30/hr

Online Tutor


An online tutor provides academic assistance and guidance to students in various subjects. They conduct virtual lessons, help with homework, and provide personalized learning support.

Average salary: $23.23/hr

Graphic Designer

A graphic designer creates visual elements for websites, advertisements, logos, and other marketing materials. They use design software to produce high-quality graphics and ensure brand consistency.

Average salary: $17-$35/hr

Web Developer

A web developer designs and creates websites using programming languages and web development tools. They are responsible for the functionality and appearance of websites, ensuring a smooth user experience.


Average salary: $20-$40/hr

E-commerce Manager

An e-commerce manager oversees online sales operations, manages product listings, and optimizes the user experience on an e-commerce platform. They analyze sales data, implement marketing strategies, and drive revenue growth.

Average salary: $23-$47/hr
