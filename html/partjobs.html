Part-time Jobs



Barista


A part-time barista works in a coffee shop or cafe, preparing and serving a variety of beverages, such as coffee, tea, and specialty drinks. They also handle cash transactions, maintain cleanliness, and provide excellent customer service.

Average salary: $8-$12/hr

Food Server

A part-time food server works in restaurants or banquet halls, taking orders, serving meals, and providing attentive customer service. They must have good communication skills, a friendly demeanor, and the ability to work well in a fast-paced environment.

Average salary: $8-$12

Personal Chef


A part-time personal chef prepares customized meals for clients in their homes. They create menus, shop for ingredients, and cook meals according to clients' preferences and dietary needs.

Average salary: $20-$40/hr

Bakery Assistant


A part-time bakery assistant supports bakers in preparing and baking a variety of bread, pastries, and desserts. They assist with ingredient measurement, dough preparation, and oven monitoring, ensuring high-quality baked goods.

Average salary: $9-$14/hr

Prep Cook

A part-time prep cook assists the kitchen team in preparing ingredients, chopping vegetables, and assembling food items. They ensure that all necessary supplies are ready for the chefs, contributing to a smooth workflow in the kitchen.

Average salary: $9-$14/hr


Food Delivery Driver:

A part-time food delivery driver transports meals from restaurants to customers' homes or offices. They ensure timely and accurate delivery while providing friendly and professional customer service. Salary Range.

Average salary: $10-$20/hr
